01 — DISCIPLINES

Six disciplines, run
from one site file.

Every project moves through the same register of drawings and sign-offs regardless of scale — so nothing gets re-explained between site visits, or between us and your contractor.

Structural Engineering

Design and certification of load-bearing systems for buildings and civil works, from raft to roof.

Geotechnical Investigation

Soil profiling, bearing capacity testing, and foundation recommendations before design begins.

Site Civil & Stormwater

Grading, drainage layout, and stormwater attenuation design for developed and paved sites.

Construction Monitoring

On-site inspection and formal sign-off through every structural milestone, not just at handover.

Project & Contract Admin

Programme, budget, and NEC/JBCC contract administration alongside the engineering scope.

Due Diligence & Audits

Condition surveys, structural audits, and remedial specifications for existing buildings.

03 — HOW A PROJECT MOVES

Site to sign-off,
in five stages.

The order is fixed by what the next stage needs from the last — you always know which stage a project is sitting in.

01

Site Assessment

Walkover, geotechnical investigation, and survey capture — establishing what the ground and the brief actually allow.

02

Concept & Modelling

Structural analysis and load cases, coordinated directly with the architectural and services teams.

03

Documentation & Approval

Construction drawings and specifications, taken through council and ECSA sign-off.

04

Construction Monitoring

Site inspection at every structural milestone, with as-built verification against the design.

05

Completion & Handover

Certificates of compliance, maintenance schedule, and a closed-out project file.
