Our Story

We build websites
that work hard.

Shoreline Web Design is a boutique web design studio founded on a simple belief: every business deserves a digital presence that genuinely represents the quality of their work.

How We Started

Built from frustration with the ordinary

We got tired of seeing talented businesses settle for generic templates and cookie-cutter agencies. The web should be a place where your brand stands apart — not blends in.

So we started Shoreline Web Design. A studio small enough to care deeply about every project, and skilled enough to execute at the highest level. No bloated teams, no account managers in the middle — just direct collaboration with the people building your site.

Every project we take on is treated like it's our own business on the line. Because to us, it is.

What Drives Us

Our core values

Intentional Design

Every visual choice has a reason. We don't add elements for decoration — we add them when they serve your audience and your goals.

Honest Partnerships

We tell you what we think, not just what you want to hear. Real advice leads to better results, and better results build lasting relationships.

Performance First

A beautiful site that loads slowly is a liability. We obsess over speed, accessibility, and technical quality from the very first line of code.

Continuous Refinement

The best work is never done. We stay curious, push our craft further, and evolve with the web so your site never feels dated.

How We Work

A clear process,
every time

01

Discovery Call

We start with a focused conversation to understand your business, your goals, your audience, and what success looks like. No intake forms — real dialogue.

02

Strategy & Scope

We define the project scope, sitemap, and strategic approach. You receive a detailed proposal before any design begins.

03

Design & Review

We present full mockups and refine based on your feedback. Two rounds of revisions are included so we get it right.

04

Build & Test

Development is clean, semantic, and tested across all major browsers and devices before you ever see it live.

05

Launch & Handover
